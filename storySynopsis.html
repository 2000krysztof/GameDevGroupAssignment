Behind the Iron Curtain

Story Sysnopsis



In an alternate 2050 USSR, Igor Volkov, a new KGB agent, investigates a spy network's collapse following a fellow agent's death. From the information found on the deceased agent’s computer, he infiltrates the Volga Whisper, discovering connections to Le Fantôme. Igor confronts Dmitri Ivanov, extracting Le Fantôme's identity through interrogation.
Sent to East Berlin, Igor blends in with rebels, discovering Mikhail Voronin's role in inciting dissent orchestrated by Le Fantôme. After apprehending Voronin, Igor unravels deeper ties to Le Fantôme. This discovery of fingerprints on a recording device brings him closer to unveiling Le Fantôme's identity, a crucial step towards confronting the enigmatic figure. Igor strikes a deal with Voronin to quell the uprising, leveraging his influence against Le Fantôme's manipulation.
In Poland, Igor investigates missing uranium and absent mining explosives. Suspecting foul play, he discreetly uncovers explosives in an unsuspecting worker's bag. Igor shadows the worker, witnessing the placement of explosives near crucial mine structures.
Upon capturing Kamil Jankowski, interrogation reveals a voice recording from Le Fantôme detailing plans for explosive sabotage and implicating the mine manager. Racing to the manager's office, Igor finds it abandoned. Stakeout at a dead drop location leads to a tense encounter with Le Fantôme, culminating in the manager's assassination and Igor facing the chilling reality of Le Fantôme's lethal capabilities.
In Vladivostok, Igor investigates a potential plot to sabotage Soviet nuclear weapons based on intercepted messages. He scouts the signal's location, struggling to identify suspects amidst a crowded area. Igor focuses on the missile site, engaging bystanders for clues.
Noticing Victor Sokolov's disinterest in sketching the missile's landscape, Igor tails him to a house, leading to an intercept of the signal. Confronting Victor with a Le Fantôme recording, Igor unravels Victor's unwitting role as an informant using paintings to transmit surveillance findings to Le Fantôme in Moscow.
Igor briefs his superiors and plans to station an agent at the missile site while he heads to Moscow, determined to capture Le Fantôme and dismantle the covert operation jeopardizing Soviet security.
In the heart of a tense confrontation within the warehouse, Igor and Le Fantôme face off amidst a precarious setting. Le Fantôme delivers a chilling warning, detailing the presence of flammable gas saturating the warehouse. With each passing moment, the tension mounts as Le Fantôme ominously reveals that any discharge of a firearm could set off a chain reaction, resulting in a catastrophic explosion that could seal Igor's fate within the confines of the warehouse.
Caught in this perilous predicament, Igor finds himself in a daunting position. He grapples with the gravity of the situation, knowing that any misstep could lead to disastrous consequences. Igor faces the daunting task of defusing the bomb, a challenge that demands utmost precision and composure in the face of imminent danger. The weight of the task at hand bears down on him as he meticulously assesses the surroundings, analyzing the bomb's mechanisms and working against the relentless ticking of the clock. Each moment feels critical as Igor focuses on neutralizing the threat, employing every ounce of skill and nerve in his arsenal to dismantle the explosive device.
Igor successfully disarms the bomb, diffusing the potential explosion that loomed large moments ago. With a surge of relief, he manages to escape the perilous environment just in the nick of time.
Regrouping outside, Igor utilizes the city's surveillance footage to track Le Fantôme's movements, his resolve unwavering despite the recent harrowing experience. As he follows the trail, Igor intercepts Le Fantôme setting up a sniper's vantage point overlooking a venue where a dictator is scheduled to speak.
Engaging in a high-stakes confrontation atop the building, Igor faces Le Fantôme's resistance. Despite Le Fantôme's attempt to turn the tables, Igor, determined and focused, engages in a tense standoff that culminates in a swift exchange of gunfire. Through skilled marksmanship Igor manages to disarm Le Fantôme, thwarting the assailant's attempts and ensuring the capture of this elusive and dangerous figure.
The chaos and commotion caused by the skirmish drew widespread media attention, broadcasting the capture of Le Fantôme and the thwarted attempt on the dictator's life. This significant event triggers a geopolitical storm, leading the dictator to declare war on the EU in response to the audacious assassination attempt orchestrated by Le Fantôme, setting the stage for intricate and far-reaching diplomatic repercussions.
